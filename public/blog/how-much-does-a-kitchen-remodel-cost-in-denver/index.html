How Much Does a Kitchen Remodel Cost in Denver? (2026 Guide)

By Red Remodels  ·  March 13, 2026

Planning a kitchen remodel in Denver? Cost is almost always the first question homeowners ask — and for good reason. A kitchen renovation is one of the biggest investments you'll make in your home.

Here's what you can realistically expect to pay in the Denver metro area in 2026.

Average Kitchen Remodel Costs in Denver

Project Size Average Cost Range
Minor refresh (paint, fixtures, hardware) $5,000 – $15,000
Mid-range remodel (new cabinets, countertops, appliances) $25,000 – $60,000
Full high-end renovation $60,000 – $120,000+

Denver labor costs run about 10–15% higher than the national average, driven by a competitive trades market and rising material costs along the Front Range.

What Drives the Cost

Cabinets (30–40% of budget)

Cabinets are typically the single largest line item. Stock cabinets are the most affordable; semi-custom and fully custom dramatically increase the price but give you exact sizing and finishes.

Countertops (10–15% of budget)

Laminate is the budget-friendly option. Quartz runs $75–$150 per square foot installed and is the most popular choice in Denver right now. Natural stone (granite, marble) costs similar but requires more maintenance.

Labor (20–35% of budget)

General contractors in Denver typically charge 15–25% of total project cost to manage the job. Specialty trades (plumber, electrician) bill $100–$175/hr.

Appliances (10–15% of budget)

Entry-level packages start around $3,000. Professional-grade appliances can push $15,000–$30,000 on their own.

How to Get the Most Value

  1. Keep the layout — moving plumbing and electrical is expensive. If you can keep the sink and range where they are, you'll save thousands.
  2. Reface instead of replace — if your cabinet boxes are solid, refacing the doors and drawers costs 30–50% less than full replacement.
  3. Time your project — January through March tends to be slower for Denver contractors, which can mean better pricing and faster scheduling.
