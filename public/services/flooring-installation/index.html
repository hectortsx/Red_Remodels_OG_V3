Flooring Installation in Denver

Whether you're replacing worn carpet in a single bedroom or installing new hardwood throughout an entire home, Red Remodels delivers a flawless result every time. We handle subfloor prep, material sourcing, installation, and trim finishing.

Colorado's dry climate and temperature swings require flooring installed with proper expansion gaps and acclimation periods. Our crew understands Denver's conditions and installs every floor to last.

Flooring Types We Install

🪵

Hardwood

Solid and engineered hardwood in oak, maple, hickory, and more. Nail-down, glue-down, and floating installations — sanded and finished on-site or pre-finished.

🏠

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Waterproof, durable, and realistic wood-look planks ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and high-traffic areas. Wide plank and herringbone patterns available.

🔲

Tile

Porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone tile for bathrooms, kitchens, entryways, and laundry rooms — installed with straight, offset, or herringbone patterns.

🌿

Laminate

Cost-effective and highly durable laminate flooring in wood-look and stone-look finishes, ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, and home offices.

🛋️

Carpet

Plush, berber, and commercial-grade carpet for bedrooms, family rooms, and staircases — installed over proper pad for comfort and longevity.

🪨

Natural Stone

Travertine, slate, marble, and limestone floors cut and installed for entryways, bathrooms, and feature areas with proper sealing and grouting.

🔧

Subfloor Prep

Leveling, patching, moisture testing, and subfloor replacement as needed before installation — the foundation of a long-lasting floor.

🎨

Hardwood Refinishing

Sand and refinish existing hardwood floors to restore their original color and sheen — far more cost-effective than full replacement.

Flooring Installation Cost in Denver

Costs vary by material type and room size. Here's what Denver homeowners typically invest (materials + labor):

  • Luxury vinyl plank (LVP)$4 – $8/sq ft installed
  • Engineered hardwood$8 – $14/sq ft installed
  • Solid hardwood (nail-down)$10 – $18/sq ft installed

Subfloor repairs, furniture moving, and stair nosing are quoted separately when needed. We provide a complete, itemized estimate before any work begins.

Ways to Save

  • Choose LVP over engineered hardwood for a similar look at lower cost
  • Refinish existing hardwood instead of replacing it
  • Combine multiple rooms into one project to reduce mobilization costs
  • Keep transitions simple — fewer thresholds and stair nosings lower material costs
