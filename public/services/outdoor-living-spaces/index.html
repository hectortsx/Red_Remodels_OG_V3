Denver's climate is one of the best in the country for outdoor living — and the right outdoor space can add significant value to your home while giving you a place to relax, entertain, and enjoy Colorado's scenery.

Red Remodels handles everything from initial design and permit submission through final construction and cleanup. Whether you want a simple Trex deck or a fully outfitted outdoor kitchen with pergola and fire pit, we build it to last Colorado's weather.

What We Build Outdoors

🪵

Decks

Pressure-treated wood, composite (Trex, TimberTech), or hardwood decks designed to fit your home's architecture and your budget. All decks are permitted and inspected.

🪨

Patios & Pavers

Concrete, flagstone, brick, and paver patios that create a durable, beautiful foundation for outdoor furniture and entertaining.

🏛️

Pergolas & Shade Structures

Freestanding or attached pergolas, shade sails, and covered patio structures that extend your outdoor season and add visual interest.

🍳

Outdoor Kitchens

Built-in grills, countertops, refrigerators, sinks, and storage — fully outfitted outdoor cooking stations for the serious entertainer.

🔥

Fire Pits & Fireplaces

Built-in gas or wood-burning fire pits and outdoor fireplaces with stone or masonry surrounds that anchor your outdoor living area.

💡

Outdoor Lighting

String lights, recessed deck lighting, path lighting, and landscape lighting to make your outdoor space usable and inviting after dark.

🌿

Retaining Walls

Boulder, block, or timber retaining walls to level sloped yards and create usable outdoor spaces on Denver's varied terrain.

🚪

Covered Entryways

Front porch additions, covered stoops, and entry canopies that improve curb appeal while protecting your door from Colorado weather.

#pricingGuide

Outdoor Living Space Cost in Denver

Costs vary based on size, materials, and complexity. Here's what Denver homeowners typically invest:

  • Basic wood or composite deck$8,000 – $25,000
  • Pergola or covered patio structure$10,000 – $35,000
  • Full outdoor kitchen & living area$30,000 – $80,000+

Denver permit fees and inspections add to project costs but ensure your structure is safe and adds to your home's resale value. We include all permit costs in our estimates.

Ways to Save

  • Choose pressure-treated wood over composite for a similar look at lower upfront cost
  • Start with a simple structure and add features (lighting, kitchen) later
  • Keep the deck shape simple — rectangular designs cost less than multi-level or curved
  • Schedule early spring for better contractor availability before summer rush
