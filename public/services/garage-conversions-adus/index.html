Garage Conversions & ADUs in Denver

Denver's ADU-friendly zoning makes garage conversions one of the most strategic home investments available. A finished ADU can generate rental income, house family members, or add a private workspace — all without expanding your footprint.

Red Remodels manages the entire process: zoning review, architectural drawings, permit submission, construction, and final inspection. We've completed conversions ranging from simple studio units to fully outfitted two-bedroom ADUs.

Free Estimate & Zoning Review

#pricingGuide

Garage Conversion Cost in Denver

Costs vary based on garage size, desired use, and finish level. Here's what Denver homeowners typically invest:

  • Basic home office or studio (no plumbing)$25,000 – $50,000
  • Guest suite with bathroom$50,000 – $90,000
  • Full ADU with kitchen & bath$80,000 – $150,000+

Denver's ADU permitting process adds cost and time but creates a legally permitted space that adds to your home's appraisal value. Rental income often offsets the investment within a few years.

Ways to Save

  • Skip the full kitchen — a kitchenette reduces plumbing cost significantly
  • Use a mini-split for HVAC instead of extending existing ductwork
  • Choose LVP flooring over tile for a durable, cost-effective finish
  • Start the permit process early — it's the longest lead-time item on the project
