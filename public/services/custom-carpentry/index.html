Ways to Save

  • Use paint-grade MDF for painted finishes — looks identical at lower cost than solid wood
  • Keep profiles simple — fewer transitions and details reduce labor time
  • Combine projects into one visit to reduce mobilization costs
  • Reuse existing hardware where condition allows
