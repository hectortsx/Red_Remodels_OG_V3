#pricingGuide

Kitchen Remodel Cost in Denver

Costs vary based on kitchen size, material choices, and scope of work. Here's what Denver homeowners typically invest:

  • Minor refresh — paint, hardware, fixtures $5,000 – $15,000
  • Mid-range remodel — new cabinets, countertops, appliances $25,000 – $60,000
  • Full high-end renovation — layout changes, custom everything $60,000 – $120,000+

Denver labor costs typically run 10–15% above the national average. We provide detailed written estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for.

Ways to Save

  • Keep your existing layout to avoid plumbing/electrical moves
  • Reface cabinet doors instead of replacing the boxes
  • Choose quartz over natural stone for lower maintenance costs
  • Schedule January–March for better contractor availability
