#pricingGuide

Bathroom Remodel Cost in Denver

Costs vary based on bathroom size, material choices, and scope of work. Here's what Denver homeowners typically invest:

  • Cosmetic refresh — fixtures, paint, vanity swap $3,000 – $10,000
  • Mid-range remodel — new tile, vanity, shower surround $12,000 – $30,000
  • Full master bath renovation — custom tile, freestanding tub, layout changes $30,000 – $75,000+

Denver labor costs typically run 10–15% above the national average. We provide detailed written estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for.

Ways to Save

  • Keep plumbing in the same location to avoid costly rerouting
  • Choose porcelain tile over natural stone for a similar look at lower cost
  • Refinish your existing tub instead of replacing it
  • Schedule January–March for better contractor availability
